Account deletion

You can delete your Elyra account.

Account deletion is available directly inside the app. This page explains the steps, what is deleted, and the contact to use if you have trouble.

Ask for help Read privacy
In-app path
  • Open Elyra.
  • Go to Profile or Settings.
  • Open Account.
  • Choose Delete account and confirm.
Steps Deleted data Retention Help

1. How to delete your account from the app

  1. Open the Elyra app on your iPhone.
  2. Go to the Profile or Settings screen.
  3. Open the Account section.
  4. Select Delete account.
  5. Read the information shown, then confirm deletion.

The exact wording may vary slightly by app version, but deletion will remain accessible from the account area.

2. What is deleted

Deletion aims to erase or anonymize data linked to your Elyra account, including account information, nutrition data, workouts, saved workout plans, community data, supplements, progress data, hydration entries, preferences, and profile elements such as a photo if one was added.

3. Possible minimal retention

Some information may be kept for a limited time if needed to comply with a legal obligation, protect service security, prevent abuse, resolve a dispute, or retain a minimal technical record of the request.

4. If you cannot access the app

If you cannot sign in or deletion does not work, email support.elyra@gmail.com with the email address linked to the account and a short description of the issue. We usually respond within 2 to 3 business days.

5. Useful links

To understand more broadly how Elyra handles data, read the Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.

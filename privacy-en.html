Privacy Policy

This policy explains, in simple terms, what data Elyra may use, why it matters to the app, and how we handle it in the V1 release.

Last updated: April 8, 2026

Public V1

The current public version does not include active ads, active subscriptions, or public AI features.

User account

Elyra supports account creation and sign-in, including Sign in with Apple when that option is available.

Contact

If you have any questions about your data or your account, you can contact us at support.elyra@gmail.com.

1. Data we may collect

Depending on how you use Elyra, we may collect certain information needed to run the service.

  • Account information, such as your email address, sign-in details, or information provided through Sign in with Apple.
  • Data you add inside the app, for example for nutrition, training, progress, or hydration.
  • Profile details, such as a display name or profile photo, if you choose to add one.
  • Messages you send if you contact support by email.

2. Why we use this data

  • Create, secure, and manage your user account.
  • Save and sync your data between sessions.
  • Display your profile and the content you log in the app.
  • Respond to your support requests.
  • Improve the stability, reliability, and performance of Elyra.

3. Storage and security

V1 data may be stored through services such as Firebase and Firestore. We use reasonable measures to help protect account access and stored data, but no online service can guarantee absolute security.

4. User account and sign-in

You may use Elyra with a user account or through Sign in with Apple when that option is offered. You remain responsible for keeping your sign-in details confidential.

5. What V1 does not include

At this stage, the public V1 of Elyra does not include active ads, active subscriptions, or public AI features.

6. Your rights

You can contact us to request access to your data, correct certain information, or request account deletion when possible and subject to any legal obligations that may apply.

7. Support contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or your data, you can write to support.elyra@gmail.com .

8. Changes to this policy

This policy may evolve as the product evolves. If an important update is made, the version published on this page will apply.

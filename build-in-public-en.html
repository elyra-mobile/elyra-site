Why this page exists

I am 20, with no coding background. I wanted a fitness app that brings everything together without scattering nutrition, training, hydration, supplements, community, and progress across several tools.

I could not find the exact app I wanted, so I started building it myself. Elyra is the result of that process: learning, testing, shipping, correcting, and improving in public.

Documenting the project keeps me honest. It makes the numbers, decisions, mistakes, and lessons visible, and it may help someone else who wants to start from zero too.
